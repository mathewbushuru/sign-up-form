One of Vancouver’s top attractions is nothing more than a paved pathway that stretches some 28 km around the city’s waterfront. But what a path it is! Known simply as “the seawall,” you’ll find that following this route is one of the best ways to discover Vancouver. The 9 km Stanley Park section is the most popular portion, and it’s easy to see why with attractions such as the Nine O'Clock Gun, the Siwash Rock, and the several beaches.
